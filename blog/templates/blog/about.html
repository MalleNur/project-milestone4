Welcome to our very own BookCircle!

We are a group of people that meet and read together!

Join our little community!

If you love to read then you are welcome to the club. We are a group of book lovers that like to read and make new friends. We usually have our meet and greets in a quite moderate places, were everybody can feel welcomed and relaxed in the enviroment. Afterwards those who wants to go to a more social setting usually take a couple of drinks or have something to eat. Everybody is welcome to join our group!

Couple of rules to know about:

  • We always meet up the second saturday every month at a place we have collectively have choosen or through a online link (Teams).
  • Everybody is welcome! The age limit is 18+.
  • If there is a book that any member wants to recomend send an email to the admin or suggest it under the meet and greet.
  • There will be a collective vote about which book that will be next. Either through email or at the end of the meeting.
